# 📊 Retail Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using Python to uncover business insights and trends. The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), data visualization, and business recommendations to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and preprocess retail sales data
- Analyze sales and profit trends
- Identify top-performing categories and regions
- Study the impact of discounts on profit
- Generate business insights and recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Google Colab

---

## 📂 Dataset

- Sample Superstore Dataset
- Records: 9,994
- Features: 21

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed

- Data Cleaning
- KPI Summary
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Sales by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Monthly Profit Trend
- Top 10 Selling Products
- Top 10 Customers
- Discount vs Profit
- Correlation Heatmap
- Sales Distribution
- Sales by Sub-Category

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
- The West region achieved the highest sales.
- Higher discounts negatively affected profitability.
- A small number of products generated a major share of total sales.
